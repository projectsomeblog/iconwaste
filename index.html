  • Sort your belongings: Separate items you want to keep, donate, or recycle.
  • Identify hazardous materials: Make note of chemicals, paint, or electrical items that require special disposal.
  • Communicate your priorities: Let the Icon Waste team know if there are items that require extra care or if there are specific areas to focus on.
  • Clear access: Ensure driveways, stairways, and doorways are accessible for efficient removal.
